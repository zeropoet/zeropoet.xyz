Profile

Mancel Lindsey is a conceptual systems artist working at the intersection of authorship and generative AI. His practice evolved from abstraction and animation into computational environments, where cyclical motion and constraint became structural inquiry. Through ØVEL, he develops explicit frameworks that formalize creative sovereignty within machine-mediated processes, treating authorship not as identity, but as infrastructure.
